# Player Management

The Z-Dashboard provides an exhaustive suite for managing your active and offline player base. Navigate to the **Players** tab via the sidebar to access the master table.

## The Player Overview Modal

Clicking on any player in the table opens their **Player Overview Modal**. This modal is the command center for interacting with an individual user.

### 1. Live Vitals
At a glance, view the player's real-time health and armor (represented by a live-updating bar), their current session ping, and Discord/Steam linked accounts.

### 2. Economy Management
Directly underneath their vitals, you can view their current **Cash** and **Bank** balances.
Click the **Edit** button next to their funds to open a secure prompt allowing you to *Set, Add, or Remove* money instantly without relying on chat commands.

### 3. Moderation Tools
The "Actions" tab provides one-click access to essential moderation features:
- **Revive / Heal**
- **Bring / Go To**
- **Spectate**
- **Freeze**
- **Warn:** Triggers an in-game UI warning popup for the user.

### 4. Danger Zone
For serious infractions, access the Danger Zone to:
- **Wipe Inventory:** Deletes all items currently on the player.
- **Kick:** Disconnects them with a custom reason.
- **Ban:** Opens the advanced Ban Dialog, allowing you to specify a reason and duration, calculating the exact unban timestamp automatically.
